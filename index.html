Green Wisdom

Plant Care Tips

Simple, actionable advice to help your plants thrive long after delivery.

💧

Watering Wisely

Most houseplants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Stick your finger 2cm into the soil — if it's dry, it's time to water. Overwatering is the #1 killer of indoor plants.

☀️

Light Requirements

Bright indirect light suits most indoor plants. Avoid harsh afternoon sun on tender leaves. North-facing windows are ideal for ferns and peace lilies. Succulents love direct sun.

🌍

Right Soil Mix

Use well-draining potting mix for most plants. Add perlite (20%) to improve aeration. Succulents and cacti need sandy, gritty soil. Orchids thrive in bark-based media.

🌡️

Temperature & Humidity

Most tropical houseplants love 18–28°C and moderate humidity. Mist leaves weekly, or place a pebble tray with water below the pot to increase humidity naturally.

🪴

When to Repot

Repot when roots start growing out of drainage holes or the plant looks too big for its pot. Spring is the best time. Choose a pot only 2–3 cm larger than the current one.

🌿

Fertilising

Feed plants during the growing season (March–September) with a balanced liquid fertiliser every 2–3 weeks. Stop fertilising in winter when plant growth naturally slows.
